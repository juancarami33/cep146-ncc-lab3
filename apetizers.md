# Homemade Guacamole #
## Ingredients ##
- avocado
- onion
- tomato
- lemon
## Steps ##
1. Cut the tomato and the onion in little pieces
1. Cut the avocados and put them inside the recipient
1. Cut the lemons and put the juice inside the recipient
1. Mix everything and eat
